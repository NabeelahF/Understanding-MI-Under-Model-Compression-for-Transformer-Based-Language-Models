Everyone had a great time at the picnic, wearing their unique hats and playing games. Lily felt happy that she could share her love of creating things with her friends. From that day on, they had many more meetings where they designed and made things together.

492. Once upon a time, there was a little boy named Timmy. Timmy loved to play outside in the park. One day, Timmy's mom told him they were going on a camping trip. Timmy was very excited! 

As they were packing, Timmy's mom reminded him to bring his flashlight. Timmy grabbed his flashlight and put it in his backpack. 

When they arrived at the campsite, Timmy wanted to explore right away. He saw a beautiful lake and wanted to go swimming. But his mom told him they needed to set up the tent first. Timmy was impatient and wanted to hurry up, but he knew he had to help his mom. 

That night, when it got dark, Timmy was glad he brought his flashlight. They used it to find their way back to the tent. Timmy realized that it was important to be patient and listen to his mom's advice. He learned that sometimes it's bitter to wait, but it's worth it in the end.

493. Once upon a time, there was a little boy named Tim. He loved to collect rocks. Every day, he would go outside and find new rocks to add to his collection. Tim had a big box by his door where he kept all his rocks. Some rocks were big, some were small, and some were very dry.

One sunny day, Tim met a new friend named Sue. Sue saw Tim collecting rocks and asked, "What are you doing?" Tim replied, "I am collecting rocks for my box by the door!" Sue thought it was fun and wanted to help Tim find more rocks.

Together, Tim and Sue looked for rocks. They found dry rocks, wet rocks, and even some shiny rocks. They put all the rocks they found in Tim's box by the door. At the end of the day, they were very happy with their new rocks. Tim and Sue became best friends and loved to collect rocks together.

494. Once upon a time, there was a little girl named Lily. She loved to paint with her fingers and make pretty pictures. One day, she went for a walk and found a lonely stone on the ground. 

Lily felt sad for the stone because it was all alone. So, she decided to paint it with pretty colors to make it happy. She used her red, blue, and yellow paint to make a rainbow on the stone. 

Now, the stone was no longer lonely. It had a friend in Lily and a pretty rainbow to look at. Lily was happy too because she made a new friend and painted something beautiful. From that day on, Lily always painted stones when she went for walks, and she never felt lonely again.

495. One day, a little girl named Emma was playing hide-and-seek with her mom. She was so great at hiding that she made her mom search for her all over the house. Finally, mom found her hiding under the bed. 

Emma thought it would be funny to try and make her nose disappear. She tried scrunching up her face and squinting her eyes really tight, but it didn't work.

So she decided to try using magic. She waved her hands around her nose and said some special words. "Vanish, disappear!," she shouted. 

And just like that, her nose disappeared! Her mom was so surprised, she almost dropped her cup of tea. Emma laughed and laughed and then her nose reappeared. 

They both had a great time playing hide-and-seek that day.

496. Once there was a boy named Fred. He was 3 years old. One day, he decided to invite his friends over for a playdate. He asked his mom for some olives to share with his friends.

Fred's mom gave him some olives and he put them on the table. He then went outside to wait for his friends to arrive.

It started to rain and soon the olives got wet. Fred was so disappointed!

Fred's friends still came over and he was really happy. He invited them to play in the rain and they had lots of fun.

After the playdate, the olives had dried out and Fred was able to eat some with his friends. They had a great time together!

497. Once upon a time there was a boy. He went for a walk in the park and he saw a tree. He wanted to climb it, but it was so high! He was a brave boy, so he tried. He put his feet on the bark and climbed, and climbed, and climbed. Suddenly, the tree began to snap! The boy jumped off quickly, and he heard a loud noise behind him. The tree had fallen over and landed in a puddle! The boy was not hurt, but he noticed something else. There was a jar of yummy sauce spilled all over the ground! He knelt down and tasted it, and it was delicious. He took it home with him and ate it with all his meals. The sauce made everything taste so much better and the boy was very happy. He was proud that his brave climb of the high tree had brought him something so special!

498. Once upon a time, there was a country. It was far away and difficult to reach. Everyone in the country was very brave. They never wanted to surrender.

One day, a giant monster attacked the country. It was so very big and strong! No one in the country knew how to fight it. They were scared and decided to surrender.

Suddenly, a magical creature arrived to help the country. It breathed magical fire from its mouth.  The monster was afraid of the magical fire and ran away.

Everyone in the country was very relieved. They cheered for the magical creature and thanked it for saving them. They knew that if the magical creature had not arrived, they would have had to surrender.

499. Once there was a little boy who loved to play in the sunshine. One day he saw a temple and wanted to go inside. He opened the door and it looked very tidy. He liked it so much he wanted to pull a door off the wall. He had heard stories about temples and thought this was OK.

So he began to pull. He was so strong. He kept going until he heard a loud snap. He felt it throughout the temple. He knew he had done something wrong but he couldn't figure out what. Suddenly he saw the wall start to topple over. He shrieked and ran out of the temple. 

The little boy was very sad. He had pulled something very important from the temple and it was all his fault. He never went to the temple again because he was too scared.

500. Once upon a time, there was a girl named Sarah. She was very excited to explore nature. Sarah was so excited that she ran quickly outside.

Sarah saw many exciting things around her. She saw mountains, trees, and birds. She was so amazed that she felt a bit dizzy. She couldn't believe how beautiful nature was!

Sarah ran around in the grass and played with the animals. She chased butterflies and laughed a lot. It was so exciting!

Eventually, Sarah got so dizzy from running around that she had to stop. She lay down and enjoyed the view of nature all around her.

Sarah felt so happy and excited about exploring nature. From that day forward, she knew that she would always love to explore the great outdoors.
